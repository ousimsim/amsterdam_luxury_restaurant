Our Story

The journey of Amsterdam Luxury Restaurant

A Dutch Culinary Haven in Morocco

Amsterdam Luxury Restaurant was born from a vision to bring the sophisticated culinary traditions of the Netherlands to the vibrant coastal city of Agadir. Founded in 2018 by Dutch restaurateur Jan van der Meer and Moroccan chef Yasmine Benali, our establishment represents the perfect marriage of European elegance and North African hospitality.

What began as a bold experiment has blossomed into one of Agadir's most prestigious dining destinations, where guests can experience the meticulous attention to detail that defines Dutch cuisine, infused with the rich flavors and warm hospitality of Morocco.

Our restaurant's design draws inspiration from Amsterdam's luxury canal houses, featuring rich dark wood, plush velvet seating, and gold accents that create an atmosphere of refined opulence.

Jan & Yasmine

Our Philosophy

Culinary Excellence

We believe in creating dishes that not only satisfy the palate but also engage all the senses. Our chefs combine traditional Dutch techniques with Moroccan flavors to create unique culinary masterpieces.

Theatrical Presentation

Dining is a performance at Amsterdam Luxury. From smoking plates to tableside preparations, we create memorable moments that elevate the dining experience beyond mere consumption.

Impeccable Service

Our staff is trained in the fine art of hospitality, combining Dutch efficiency with Moroccan warmth to ensure that every guest feels valued and attended to throughout their visit.
